# Socializing

Handles: event invitations (parties, dinners, weddings, celebrations); social gatherings Kieran is hosting or attending; RSVPs and responses to invitations; birthday reminders; social commitments with a date (e.g. "let's grab dinner on the 15th"); follow-up obligations from social events (thank-you notes, returning a borrowed item).

Does **not** handle: gifts being ordered or tracked (→ gifting or shipment-tracking); medical or financial appointments (→ healthcare or finance); work meetings or professional events.
