# Finance

Handles: credit card statements and activity; bank account statements and alerts; payment due notices; subscription charges and renewals; utility bills; account balance notifications; tax documents (1099s, W-2s); investment and brokerage statements; equity notifications (EquityZen, Carta); debt or past-due notices; any email from a financial institution, lender, or billing department; business expense tracking when Kieran explicitly marks something as a business expense.

Does **not** handle: medical bills or insurance EOBs (→ healthcare); car insurance (→ car-maintenance); package shipping charges (→ shipment-tracking).
